PROBLEM DOMAIN

Local musicians and performers are struggling to gain visibility and support in many communities. These individuals often lack the platform to showcase their talents and work, and they find it difficult to collaborate with other potential artists. Despite the existing platforms such as Instagram or SoundCloud, it is still difficult for them to stand out due to the highly saturated and competitive nature of the industry. This situation not only hinders local artists from gaining exposure but also deprives communities of enriching cultural experiences and the opportunity to support their local economy. The problems, however, extend beyond mere visibility, they include the difficulty of forming meaningful connections and collaborations in the ecosystem of local artists. They are struggling to find opportunities that allow them to share their work properly whilst engaging with collaborators, and people of interest. Addressing these challenges would be crucial to nurturing a more vibrant, supportive, and collective community where artists can flourish and enrich the cultural fabric of their communities.

APP DESCRIPTION

This web app aims to level the playing field for music artists from minority groups, prioritizing inclusivity and accessibility. It does not only probe as a platform for the artists but also to those seeking for talent. It is bound to enhance economic empowerment, community networking, educational opportunities, and enhanced accessibility. This web app will offer 2 main features and a minor feature to engage with its users. The first feature is the Collaboration and Crowdfunding platform, where artists can publish their work to look for funding or collaboration. Other users can explore these projects, make financial contributions, or search for talent. In addition to fostering artistic partnerships, this platform makes community-supported funding possible, enabling artists to complete projects that otherwise might not have been feasible owing to budgetary limitations. The second feature that we are planning to implement is called “Masterclasses Sessions With Well-known Artists”. End-users who are interested in booking an appointment with a renown-artist from various fields and intend to learn from their talents and gain more opportunities. We plan to incorporate some sort of list of artists and the variety of disciplines that they teach. Users can then click to book an appointment, or also move the specific artist to another list of ‘favorite artists’. Overall, this section seeks to offer inspiration, skill development, and educational value by offering special learning opportunities and insights into the professional world of art, bridging the gap between local talent and seasoned industry experts.

Accessibility Features for Users with Disabilities:

The call for heightened awareness regarding accessibility in web design is important. Universal design principles are essential in crafting digital platforms that cater inclusively to all users, including those with disabilities. By embedding these principles, we create an environment where artists of all abilities can thrive equally, thus enriching the tapestry of our musical culture with diverse expressions. Initiatives that prioritize these values may begin by addressing the needs of the visually impaired. Such measures would include ensuring compatibility with screen readers, offering high contrast modes, and providing options to adjust text size, thus accommodating individuals with varying degrees of visual ability. This approach not only levels the playing field for artists with physical challenges but also signifies a stride toward a more empathetic and wide-ranging creative community.
